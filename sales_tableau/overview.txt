Key Requirements

- KPI Overview
    display a summary of total sales, profits and quantity of the current year and the pervious year.
- Sales Trends (sparkline charts)
    present the data for each KPI on a monthly basis for both the current and pervious year
    identify months with highest and lowest sales and make them easy to recognize
- Product Subcategory Comparison (bar in bar chart)
    compare sales performance by different product subcategories for current and pervious year
    include a comparison of sales with profit 
- Weekly trends for the sales and profit (line chart)
    present weekly sales and profit data of the current year 
    display the average weekly values
    highlight weeks that are above and below the average to draw attention to sales and profit performance

Dynamic Dashboard
- The dashboard should allow users to check historical data by offering them the flexibility to select any desired year.
- Provide users with the ability to navigate between the dashboard easily
- Make the charts and graphs interactive, enabling the users to filter data using charts.
